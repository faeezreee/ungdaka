Vacuum Pump Oils

Low Volatility High Vacuum Long Life

Product Description

Our premium Vacuum Pump Oils are specially formulated to meet the demanding requirements of modern vacuum pumps. These high-performance lubricants feature exceptional oxidation stability, low vapor pressure, and superior moisture separation properties to ensure optimal vacuum pump efficiency and reliability. Their unique formulation prevents the formation of sludge and deposits while providing excellent protection against wear and corrosion.

Key Features

  • Exceptional oxidation stability
  • Ultra-low vapor pressure
  • Superior moisture separation
  • Excellent wear protection
  • Advanced sludge prevention
  • Extended service intervals
  • High thermal stability

Applications

  • Rotary vane vacuum pumps
  • Diffusion pumps
  • Mechanical booster pumps
  • Industrial vacuum systems
  • Laboratory vacuum equipment
  • Process vacuum pumps
  • High vacuum applications
