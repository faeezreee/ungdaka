CNC-OS Oil Skimmer (Korea)

Efficient CNC Specific Korean Quality

Product Description

The CNC-OS Oil Skimmer, manufactured in Korea, is a specialized oil removal system designed specifically for CNC machine applications. This compact and efficient device continuously removes tramp oils from coolant systems, helping maintain coolant quality and extend coolant life. Its precise engineering ensures reliable operation in demanding CNC environments while requiring minimal maintenance.

Key Features

  • Compact design for CNC machines
  • Continuous operation capability
  • High removal efficiency
  • Easy installation
  • Low maintenance requirements
  • Durable construction
  • Energy efficient operation

Applications

  • CNC machine sumps
  • Coolant tanks
  • Machine tool reservoirs
  • Parts washing systems
  • Cutting fluid maintenance
  • Process fluid cleaning
  • Industrial parts washers
