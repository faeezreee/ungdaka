Slideway Oils

Stick-Slip Prevention Smooth Operation Machine Tool Protection

Product Description

Our Slideway Oils are premium lubricants specifically formulated for machine tool slideways and guides. These specialized oils provide exceptional adhesion to metal surfaces, superior stick-slip prevention, and excellent load-carrying capacity. They ensure smooth, precise movement of machine tool carriages, tables, and headstocks while protecting against wear and corrosion.

Key Features

  • Excellent adhesion to metal surfaces
  • Superior stick-slip prevention properties
  • High load-carrying capacity
  • Exceptional water separation
  • Corrosion and wear protection
  • Compatible with cutting fluids
  • Long-lasting performance

Applications

  • Machine tool slideways and guides
  • CNC machine tools
  • Horizontal and vertical guides
  • Lathes and milling machines
  • Precision grinding machines
  • Industrial machinery tables
  • Manufacturing equipment
