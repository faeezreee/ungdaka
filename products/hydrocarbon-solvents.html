Hydrocarbon Solvents

High Purity Versatile Industrial Grade

Product Description

Our Hydrocarbon Solvents are refined blends formulated for effective cleaning, degreasing and use as formulation solvents in industrial processes. They offer controlled evaporation rates and strong solvency for oils, greases and similar contaminants.

Key Features

  • High solvency power for oils and greases
  • Controlled evaporation rates
  • Available in bulk and drum pack sizes
  • Consistent quality and low impurity levels

Applications

  • Industrial degreasing
  • Cleaning and surface preparation
  • Formulation solvent for coatings and adhesives
  • General maintenance cleaning
