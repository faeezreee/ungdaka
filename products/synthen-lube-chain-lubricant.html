Synthen Lube Chain Lubricant

Synthetic High Temperature Long Life

Product Description

Synthen Lube Chain Lubricant is an advanced synthetic lubricant specifically formulated for the demanding requirements of industrial chains. This high-performance formula combines superior penetration with excellent adhesion to provide lasting protection under extreme conditions. Its unique formulation ensures minimal fling-off, reduced wear, and extended chain life even in high-temperature environments.

Key Features

  • Advanced synthetic formulation
  • Excellent high-temperature stability (-30°C to 250°C)
  • Superior wear protection
  • Minimal fling-off
  • Extended relubrication intervals
  • Fast penetration properties
  • Excellent corrosion protection
  • Non-staining formula

Applications

  • Roller chains
  • Conveyor chains
  • Drive chains
  • High-temperature ovens
  • Paint drying systems
  • Textile machinery
  • Food processing equipment
