Supertap Cutting Lubricant

Precision Threading Tool Life Multi-Metal

Product Description

Supertap Cutting Lubricant is a specialized cutting fluid formulated for precision threading and tapping operations. This high-performance lubricant ensures superior tool life and exceptional thread quality across a wide range of metals.

Key Features

  • Superior cooling and lubrication properties
  • Extends tool life significantly
  • Reduces friction and heat generation
  • Compatible with multiple metal types
  • Easy application and cleanup
  • Prevents thread damage and galling

Applications

  • Threading operations
  • Tapping operations
  • CNC machining
  • Manual machining processes
  • Production line threading
