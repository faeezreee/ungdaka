Tapping Fluids

High Performance Thread Quality Extended Tool Life

Product Description

Our Tapping Fluids are specially formulated cutting fluids designed for superior performance in tapping and threading operations. These high-performance fluids provide excellent lubricity and cooling properties, ensuring precise thread formation and extended tool life. The advanced formulation prevents chip welding and ensures smooth operation in both through-hole and blind-hole tapping applications.

Key Features

  • Superior lubricity
  • Excellent cooling properties
  • Prevents chip welding
  • Extended tap life
  • Precise thread formation
  • Reduced torque requirements
  • Clean running formula

Applications

  • Through-hole tapping
  • Blind-hole tapping
  • Thread forming
  • Thread cutting
  • CNC tapping operations
  • Manual tapping
  • Various metal types
