Compressor Oils

High Performance Low Carbon Long Life

Product Description

Our premium Compressor Oils are specially formulated to meet the demanding requirements of modern air compressors. These high-performance lubricants deliver exceptional oxidation stability, minimal carbon formation, and superior wear protection, ensuring reliable operation and extended service intervals.

Key Features

  • Excellent oxidation stability
  • Low carbon formation
  • Superior wear protection
  • Rapid air release
  • Extended drain intervals
  • Excellent rust prevention

Applications

  • Rotary screw compressors
  • Reciprocating compressors
  • Rotary vane compressors
  • Centrifugal compressors
  • Oil-flooded compressors
  • Oil-free compressors
