ECC (Electro Contact Cleaner)

Fast Evaporating Non-Conductive Precision Cleaning

Product Description

ECC (Electro Contact Cleaner) is a specialized, fast-evaporating cleaning solution designed for precision cleaning of electronic and electrical components. This professional-grade formula leaves no residue and is completely non-conductive, making it safe for use on sensitive electronic equipment. Its rapid evaporation properties ensure minimal downtime during maintenance procedures.

Key Features

  • Rapid evaporation formula
  • Non-conductive properties
  • Leaves no residue
  • Safe for sensitive electronics
  • Removes oil, grease, and contaminants
  • Precise spray application

Applications

  • Electronic circuit boards
  • Electrical contacts and switches
  • Precision instruments
  • Control panels
  • Electric motors and generators
  • Communication equipment

Benefits

  • Minimizes equipment downtime
  • Improves electrical contact reliability
  • Prevents electrical shorts
  • Extends component life
  • Safe for most plastics and insulation
