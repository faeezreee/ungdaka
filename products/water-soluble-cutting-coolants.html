Water Soluble Cutting Coolants

Eco-Friendly High Performance Long Life

Product Description

Our Water Soluble Cutting Coolants are advanced metalworking fluids designed for superior performance in various machining operations. These environmentally friendly coolants provide excellent cooling and lubrication properties while being safe for operators and the environment. The advanced formulation ensures long sump life, excellent corrosion protection, and superior biological stability.

Key Features

  • Superior cooling properties
  • Excellent lubrication
  • Bio-stable formulation
  • Extended sump life
  • Corrosion protection
  • Low foaming
  • Operator friendly

Applications

  • General machining
  • Grinding operations
  • Milling processes
  • Drilling operations
  • Turning applications
  • CNC machining
  • Various metal types
